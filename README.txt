Overview..

A simple and dynamic JavaScript quiz app! Choose from three categories, earn scores, and collect medals. Get instant feedback, replay levels, and enjoy a fun learning experience.


Key Features:

Diverse Categories: Choose from three distinct categories to tailor your quiz experience.

Dynamic Scoring System: Rack up points as you progress through each category, with a dedicated score for every section. Challenge yourself to achieve the highest score possible and showcase your mastery in each domain.

Total Quiz Score: The competition heats up as you accumulate your total quiz score, reflecting your overall knowledge prowess. 

Medal Bonanza: Conquer each category to earn prestigious medals based on your scores. From bronze to silver and gold, these medals are a testament to your intellectual achievements. 

Interactive Feedback: Receive instant feedback after each question, indicating whether your answer is correct. If you stumble, fear not! it provides the correct answer, turning every mistake into a learning opportunity.

Seamless Navigation: Navigate through the quiz at your pace. Choose to replay a level, advance to the next category, or start anew with a different topic. The power is in your hands to shape your quiz experience.
